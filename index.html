CareGuard App

Healthcare Financial App

The initial CareGuard app was designed and developed during a hackathon event. Many members of the development team work remotely from all over the country, this was a chance for everyone to get together and work on something together in the same space. I accompanied the development team to assist with the design for the app.

The app allows users to sign in, view their account information, see recent transactions, their current balance amount, and have access to their CareGuard card.

Public Foods

Community Cookbook

Public Foods is an online cookbook that hosts recipes from my friends and family. I'm not alone with my love for cooking, so I decided to create a site that allows my friends to create an account and share their favorite recipes.

Many of the cooking sites around the web are cluttered and have too much filler content. I wanted Public Foods to have a minimal design that focuses on the recipes.
